Pine City Mall

Pine Crest Centre’s renovations are well underway and the centre is in a state of transformation.Shoppers at Pine Crest will soon see a premier shopping centre emerging with an extra 6000sqm of new retail area.

The scope of work includes; improving aesthetics both interior and exterior, upgraded ablution facilities, new and refurbished escalators, new lighting and flooring. Once tiling of the mall is nearing completion, new ceilings and some shop fronts will be installed; On completion of the project, the centre will host new features such as a contemporary and urban food court area including a kiddie’s play area.
